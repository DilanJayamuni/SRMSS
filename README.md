# Smart Route Management and Scheduling System (SRMSS)

## Overview

The **Smart Route Management and Scheduling System (SRMSS)** is a centralized digital platform designed to modernize and optimize public transport depot operations. The system addresses inefficiencies caused by manual record-keeping and spreadsheet-based scheduling by providing intelligent route planning, vehicle allocation, driver management, and operational monitoring capabilities.

SRMSS aims to improve fleet utilization, reduce scheduling conflicts, enhance operational visibility, and support data-driven decision-making within public transport depots.

---

## Problem Statement

Public transport depots in Sri Lanka primarily rely on manual processes for managing routes, schedules, drivers, and vehicle assignments. These traditional methods often lead to:

* Route overlaps and scheduling conflicts
* Delayed departures and service disruptions
* Inefficient utilization of buses and drivers
* Limited visibility into operational performance
* Difficulty maintaining fuel and maintenance records
* Lack of centralized reporting and analytics

These challenges impact operational efficiency, service quality, and resource management.

---

## Proposed Solution

SRMSS provides a centralized web-based platform that automates route planning, scheduling, fleet management, and reporting activities.

The system enables depot administrators and supervisors to manage transport operations through a single dashboard while ensuring optimal resource allocation and operational transparency.

By digitizing existing workflows, SRMSS improves productivity, reduces manual errors, and promotes sustainable transport operations without requiring additional fleet expansion.

### Primary Objectives

* Improve maintenance tracking

---

## Key Features

### Route Planning Module

* Create and manage transport routes
* Define route start points, destinations, and intermediate stops
* Assign drivers and vehicles based on availability and capacity
* Visualize routes using map integration services
* Manage route modifications and updates

### Schedule Management

* Create daily, weekly, and monthly schedules
* Automatically detect scheduling conflicts
* Prevent route overlaps and resource conflicts
* Support emergency schedule adjustments
* Manage departure and arrival times

### Depot Management Dashboard

* Monitor active routes and trips
* View driver and vehicle allocations
* Track operational status in real time
* Display route performance metrics
* Monitor fleet utilization rates

### Fuel and Maintenance Management

* Record fuel consumption for each vehicle
* Track maintenance activities and service history
* Monitor vehicle health and service schedules
* Generate maintenance and fuel reports
* Identify inefficient vehicle usage patterns

### Driver Management

* Maintain driver profiles
* Track license validity
* Manage working hours and assignments
* View route allocation history
* Monitor driver availability

### Vehicle Management

* Maintain vehicle information records
* Track registration details and seating capacity
* Store mileage information
* Monitor maintenance schedules
* Manage vehicle availability

### Reporting and Analytics

* Generate operational reports
* Analyze trip completion rates
* Monitor fuel consumption trends
* Track route performance
* Export reports in PDF format
* Support management decision-making through analytics
